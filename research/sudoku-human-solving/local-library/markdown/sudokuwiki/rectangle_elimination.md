# SudokuWiki Rectangle Elimination

Source ID: `SUDOKUWIKI-RECTANGLE-ELIMINATION`

URL: https://www.sudokuwiki.org/Rectangle_Elimination

Accessed: 2026-06-23

Local research summary:

- Released 14 Oct 2023; updated through 2026.
- Presented by SudokuWiki as a Tough strategy and as a replacement for Empty Rectangles.
- The page states the pattern can also be expressed with AICs, usually with at least one grouped cell.
- Core example: digit 9, hinge `G2`, strong wing `G6`, weak wing `A2`; if `A2` is on then `G6` is also on, and both remove all 9s from the fourth-corner box, so `A2<>9`.
- Multiple-elimination exemplar from start: `200709006190000002080002030070503040000000000050904060060300090800000053900407001`.
