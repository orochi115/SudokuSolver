# Turbot Family / 多宝鱼家族

## One-Sentence Rule

Short single-digit chains prove one of two endpoints must be true, eliminating the digit from cells seeing both endpoints.

## Named Forms

- Skyscraper / 摩天楼.
- 2-String Kite / 双线风筝.
- Turbot Fish / 多宝鱼.
- Empty Rectangle / 空矩形.

## Human Scan Procedure

1. Pick one digit.
2. Find conjugate pairs, where the digit appears exactly twice in a house.
3. Connect two strong links with a weak link.
4. Identify endpoints that cannot both be false.
5. Remove the digit from cells seeing both endpoints.
