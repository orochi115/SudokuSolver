# Uniqueness Techniques / 唯一性技巧

## One-Sentence Rule

If a candidate choice would create a pattern allowing multiple solutions, eliminate that choice under the assumption the puzzle has a unique solution.

## Important Assumption

Uniqueness is not a basic Sudoku rule. Use these techniques only when the puzzle is known or assumed to have exactly one solution.

## Main Patterns

- Unique Rectangle / 唯一矩形.
- Avoidable Rectangle.
- Hidden Rectangle.
- BUG and BUG+1 / 全双值格致死.

## Human Scan Procedure

1. Look for two rows and two columns spanning exactly two boxes.
2. Check whether four cells nearly contain the same two candidates.
3. Identify extra candidates that prevent a deadly pattern.
4. Eliminate candidates that would force the deadly pattern.
