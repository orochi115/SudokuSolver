# Bibliography and Source Notes

Access date for this local library expansion: 2026-06-01.

## Source Groups

### HoDoKu

- Source ID prefix: `HODOKU-*`
- Site: https://hodoku.sourceforge.net/en/techniques.php
- Author/site: Bernhard Hobiger / HoDoKu
- License note found on pages: GNU FDLv1.3
- Local Markdown: `local-library/markdown/hodoku/`
- Local raw HTML: `local-library/raw-html/hodoku/`
- Coverage: terminology, singles, intersections, subsets, fish, wings, coloring, chains, ALS, uniqueness, Sue de Coq, last resort.
- Use: primary formal source.

- Site: https://www.sudokuwiki.org/strategy_families
- Author/site: Andrew Stuart / SudokuWiki.org
- Copyright note on pages: text is copyrighted by Andrew Stuart / Syndicated Puzzles; permission may be required for reproduction.
- Local Markdown: `local-library/markdown/sudokuwiki/`
- Local raw HTML: `local-library/raw-html/sudokuwiki/`
- Coverage: strategy families, logical vs brute force, intersections, subsets, X-Wing, Y-Wing, Swordfish, coloring, AIC, uniqueness, ALS.
- Supplemental pages added later: XY-Chains, X-Cycles, 3D Medusa, W-Wing, XYZ-Wing, BUG, Forcing Nets, AIC with Groups, AIC with ALSs, and AIC with Unique Rectangles.

## Copyright Handling

- HoDoKu and Sudopedia are identified as GNU FDL sources in their pages.
- SudokuWiki, Sudoku of the Day, Sudoku.com, Sudoku Bliss, and some Chinese pages have unclear or restrictive copyright terms. They are saved locally for research/reference with source attribution. Downstream public materials should quote sparingly, paraphrase, and cite source URLs.
