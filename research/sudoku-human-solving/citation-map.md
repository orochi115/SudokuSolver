## Citation Style

Use inline source lists in technique cards:

`Sources: HODOKU-BASIC-FISH, SUDOKUWIKI-XWING, SUDOKUCOM-ZH-XWING`

When drafting public-facing prose, cite URLs from `bibliography.md` rather than local file paths.
