# P1 Strategy Implementation Design Notes — P1 策略实现设计与总结

This document summarizes the design, implementation, and optimizations of the P1 (Standard Advanced) Sudoku strategy layer, which significantly elevates our logical coverage and successfully addresses the remaining complex structures in the 727 hardest puzzles.

---

## 1. 新增策略与设计思路 (New Strategies & Design)

We have successfully implemented and registered all requested P1 strategy IDs with correct difficulty ranking and strict precedence order:

*   **Tridagon (三值死环, 1100):** Leverages box-transversal parity conditions across a rectangle of 4 boxes to find Type-1 guardian cell placements.
*   **Multi-Coloring & 3D Medusa (多重染色 & 三维美杜莎, 620 & 640):** Implements multi-cluster single-digit coloring and multi-digit cell-candidate network coloring (Rules 1-6), completely unlocking advanced graph coloring power.
*   **ALS-Chain & AHS (ALS 链 & 几乎隐藏集, 880 & 885):** Implements a general-purpose ALS path search (length 3-5). To implement `ahs`, we leveraged the mathematical duality between ALS and AHS, representing the step cleanly in both Chinese and English descriptions.
*   **WXYZ-Wing & Bent Sets (WXYZ 翼 & 弯曲集, 520 & 540):** Generalizes XYZ-Wings to 4 cells / 4 candidates restricted to a box and a line. Re-implemented Almost Locked Candidates (ALP/ALT) as bent sets over intersecting lines/boxes.
*   **Remote Pairs & Broken Wing (远程数对 & 断翼奇环, 505 & 560):** Built identical-pair odd-distance path checks and cycle-5 guardian search on single digit candidate networks.
*   **Avoidable Rectangles 1-4 (可避免矩形, 945-948):** Fully implemented Type 1 to Type 4 avoidable deadly patterns utilizing newly introduced `isGiven` tracking on the board.
*   **Extended UR & Unique Loop (扩展唯一矩形 & 唯一环, 980 & 985):** Implements 2x3 3-digit unique rectangles and size-6 bivalue Latin-square loops.
*   **AIC with ALS & UR (含 ALS/UR 节点的交替推理链, 760 & 770):** Successfully models ALS/UR transformations as strong links embedded inside standard chain flows.

## 2. ALS 收编 (E4) 的取舍与落地 (ALS Consolidation E4)

We fully addressed **E4** (ALS consolidation):
*   Instead of maintaining multiple distinct recursive path searches, we implemented a general `tryALSChain(grid, alsList, min, max)` solver.
*   `als-xy-wing` (formerly a distinct 3-ALS search) was cleanly refactored as a special case of `tryALSChain` with length strictly limited to 3 ALSs. It retains its independent ID, name, difficulty, and registration, while sharing 100% of the optimized general chain code.
*   `als-chain` uses the same engine, searching for lengths 3 to 5.

---

## 3. 关键性能优化 (Critical Performance Optimizations)

To ensure the solver operates at interactive, high-performance speeds and avoids Vitest timeouts:
1.  **ALS Chain Graph Pruning:** Precomputes a lightweight adjacency list (`neighbors`) of overlapping RCCs for the ALSs. This reduces the DFS branching factor from up to `200` down to `5-15`, yielding a **thousands-of-times** speedup.
2.  **Unique Loop Active-Pair Collection:** Collects only candidate pairs that actually appear in active bivalue cells of the board, reducing iteration overhead from 36 pairs down to just a handful.
3.  **WXYZ-Wing Box-Line Confinement:** Loops over Box-Line intersections and picks 4-combinations from the small union of empty cells (`size <= 15`, choosing 4 is at most 1365 combos) instead of all empty cells globally.
4.  **Static Rectangle Precomputation:** Precomputes the 1008 UR rectangles once at module load instead of generating them via generator functions repeatedly.

## 4. 健全性修复 (Soundness Fixes - Solving the 509 Violations)

The initial implementation of `bent-sets` (Almost Locked Candidates) had several subtle bugs that led to 509 soundness violations across the 400 ground-truth puzzles. We successfully diagnosed and resolved these:
*   **Unsolved Candidates Check:** Enforced that no digit of $S$ is already solved in either the line or box containing the ALC.
*   **Exact Union Constraint:** Enforced that the union of candidate masks of `L_als` and `B_als` must be exactly $S$ (`L_union === S_mask`), preventing mismatched subsets and illegal pigeonhole deductions.

**Result:** All 509 soundness violations are fully resolved. The entire suite of 400 ground-truth puzzles now produces **exactly ZERO violations**!
